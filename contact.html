Frequently Asked Questions

How do I access my certificate?

After successfully completing a course and passing the test, you can immediately print your certificate. You'll also receive a copy via email for your records.

Are your courses accredited?

Yes! We are approved by ASWB, NBCC, and recognized by NYSED. Please see our Accreditation section for complete details.

Can I preview courses before purchasing?

Absolutely! All our courses can be viewed for free with no registration required. You only pay after you pass the test.

What if I have technical difficulties?

Contact us immediately via phone or email. Our support team is ready to help you resolve any technical issues quickly.
